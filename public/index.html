Technical Leadership

Over time, I’ve learned that leadership in engineering rarely looks dramatic. It shows up in architecture reviews. In naming decisions. In execution ordering. In asking uncomfortable questions about long-term consequences.

I push for systems that are observable. Workflows that are explicit. Tests that enforce discipline. And decisions that are documented instead of tribal.

My ambition is not just to build working software — but to shape technical direction in ways that reduce complexity for everyone who builds after me.
